Blacklist Terms and Conditions

Welcome to our Blacklist Terms page. Below you will find important information regarding the terms that are associated with being placed on the blacklist. This page also explains the conditions under which a term can be considered for blacklisting and when it cannot be canceled.

What is a Blacklist?

The blacklist is a list of individuals, companies, or entities that are restricted from certain privileges or services due to specific reasons outlined in our terms and conditions. Being on the blacklist is a serious matter and can have long-lasting effects.

Terms that Cannot Be Canceled

Once a term has been placed on the blacklist, it cannot be canceled or removed.

Reasons for Being Added to the Blacklist

An entity or individual may be added to the blacklist for the following reasons:

  • Being an idiot: Being a general idiot.
  • Violence: Engaging in violent activities.
  • Selfishnes: Being selfish and not showing car to anyone.
  • Bullying: Repeatedly disregarding the community rules or guidelines.

Note: While most terms are permanent, certain terms may be reviewed for possible removal from the blacklist after a thorough investigation, provided sufficient evidence is presented to support a change in status.

Review and Appeal Process

If you believe you have been wrongfully added to the blacklist, you may request a review of your case. Please provide any supporting documentation or evidence that you believe will aid in the review process. Appeals are reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and a final decision will be communicated to you within a specified time frame.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding the blacklist or wish to discuss your status, please contact our support team at blacklist-support@mas0ng.com.
